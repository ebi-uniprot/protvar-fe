# <a id="population"></a>Population

The **Population Observation** panel shows what's known about the variant — and other variants at the same amino acid — in public databases.

![Population Observation panel](images/pop_ann1.png)

## Top of the panel

- **Submitted Variant** — your variant with its gnomAD allele frequency and a rarity label (e.g. *very rare*, *rare*, *common*). The full HGVS notation appears alongside.
- **Co-located Variants at Residue Level** — other [co-located variants](#glossary:co-located-variants) reported at the *same amino acid position* but caused by different nucleotide changes. Click any entry to expand its details below.

## Variant details

Selecting a variant from either panel above expands its full record:

- **Cross references** — identifiers in UniProt, ClinVar, ClinGen, dbSNP, gnomAD and TOPMed, with clinical-significance tags (e.g. *pathogenic*) where reported.
- **Population frequencies** — minor allele frequency (MAF) and any population-stratified frequencies that are available.
- **Associated diseases** — disease descriptions linked to the variant, with PubMed and ClinVar evidence references.

Co-located variants share the residue position but may have different consequences — both are useful context for interpreting the submitted variant.
